# Privacy Policy for AutoFill Job Applications

**Last Updated: March 12, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
This extension is designed to help users automate the process of filling job application forms. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
- **Local Storage**: All profile data, including resume details and application history, is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API.
- **No Background Collection**: The extension does not collect or transmit your data in the background without your knowledge.
- **AI Integration**: When you use AI features (like "Generate Cover Letter" or "Single AI Fill"), only the relevant portion of your local profile data and the job description from the active tab are sent to the AI provider (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) to generate the requested content. 

## 3. Third-Party Services
- This extension uses AI APIs. Your data is subject to the privacy policies of these providers:
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)
  - [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

## 4. Permissions
- **activeTab**: Used only to interact with the job application form you are currently viewing.
- **storage**: Used to save your resume profiles locally.
- **sidePanel**: Used to provide a user interface for managing profiles and viewing application status.

## 5. Your Rights
You can delete your data at any time by clearing the extension's local storage or uninstalling the extension.

## 6. Contact
For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
